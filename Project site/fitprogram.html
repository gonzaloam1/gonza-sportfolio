12-Week Workout Program

Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4): Hypertrophy and Fat Burn

Monday: Glutes and Legs (High Intensity)

  • Hip thrust with kettlebell (14 kg): 4x15-20 reps
    • Superset With
  • Walking lunges with kettlebell (12 kg each hand): 4x12 steps per leg

  • Romanian deadlift with kettlebell (12 kg): 4x12 reps
    • Superset With
  • Goblet squat with kettlebell (14 kg): 4x15 reps

  • Bodyweight hip abduction: 4x20 reps

  • HIIT Finisher: Jump rope sprints: 30 sec work / 15 sec rest, 10 rounds

Tuesday: Chest and Arms (Supersets for Hypertrophy)

  • Kettlebell chest press (14 kg): 4x10 reps
    • Superset With
  • Incline push-ups: 4x until failure

  • Bent-over kettlebell row (12 kg): 4x12 reps per arm
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell bicep curls (12 kg): 4x12 reps

  • Dips on bar/chair: 4x until failure
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell tricep extensions (8 kg): 4x12-15 reps

Wednesday: Glutes and Abs (Core Focus)

  • Kettlebell glute bridge (12 kg): 4x15 reps
    • Superset With
  • Bodyweight glute kickbacks: 4x15 per leg

  • Plank with leg raise: 4x30 seconds per leg
    • Superset With
  • Mountain climbers: 4x30 seconds

  • 15-20 min HIIT Circuit: Jump rope + Side planks + Fast squats

Thursday: Back and Arms (Strength Focus)

  • Assisted pull-ups: 4x until failure

  • Kettlebell rows (14 kg): 4x12 reps per arm
    • Superset With
  • Concentrated bicep curl (8 kg): 4x12 reps

  • Kettlebell tricep kickbacks (8 kg): 4x12-15 reps
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell lateral raises (6-8 kg): 4x12-15 reps

Friday: Full Body Metabolic Circuit

  • Kettlebell Clean & Press (12 kg): 4x10 reps

  • Explosive bodyweight lunges: 4x20 reps

  • Push-ups with shoulder taps: 4x until failure

  • Kettlebell rows (14 kg): 4x12 reps

  • Burpees with jump: 4x12 reps

Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8): Hormonal Stimulation and Definition

Monday: Legs and Glutes (Explosive Focus)

  • Jump squats: 4x15 reps
    • Superset With
  • Single-leg Romanian deadlift (8 kg): 4x12 per leg

  • Jump lunges: 4x20 reps
    • Superset With
  • Explosive hip thrusts with kettlebell: 4x15 reps

  • HIIT Jump Rope: 30 sec work / 15 sec rest, 10 rounds

Tuesday: Chest and Arms (High Repetition)

  • Diamond push-ups: 4x until failure
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell press (12 kg): 4x15 reps

  • Kettlebell row (12 kg): 4x20 reps
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell bicep curls (12 kg): 4x15 reps

  • Dips: 4x until failure

Wednesday: Glutes and Abs (Core and Isolation)

  • Kettlebell glute bridge (12 Kg): 4x20 reps
    • Superset With
  • Bodyweight glute kickbacks: 4x15 per leg

  • Plank with leg raise: 4x30 seconds per leg
    • Superset with
  • Mountain climbers: 4x30 seconds

  • HIIT Circuit: 15-20 minutes (jump Rope, Fast Squats, Side Planks)

Thursday: Back and Arms (Strenght Focus)

  • Assisted pull-ups: 4x until failure

  • Kettlebell rows (14 kg): 4x12 reps per arm
    • Superset with
  • Concentrated biceps curls (8kg):4x12 reps

  • Kettlebell tricep kickbacks (8 kg):4x12-15 reps
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell lateral raises (6-8 kg):4x12-15 reps

Friday: Full Body Metabolic Circuit

  • Kettlebell clean & press (12 kg): 4x10 reps

  • Explosive bodyweight lunges: 4x20 reps

  • Concentrated biceps curls (8kg):4x12 reps

  • Push-ups with shoulder taps: 4x until failure

  • Kettlebell rows (14 kg): 4x12 reps

  • Burpees with jump: 4x12 reps

Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12): Maximum Fat Burn and Final Definition

Monday: Glutes and Legs (High Intensity)

  • Jump squats: 4x20 reps
    • Superset With
  • Hip thrusts with kettlebell (14 kg): 4x15-20 reps

  • Walking lunges with kettlebell (8 kg each hand): 4x12 steps per leg
    • Superset With
  • Single-leg Romanian deadlift (12 kg): 4x12 per leg

  • HIIT Jump Rope + Fast squats: 30 sec work / 15 sec rest, 10 rounds

Tuesday: Chest and Arms (Resistance and Definition)

  • Decline push-ups: 4x until failure
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell chest press (14 kg): 4x12-15 reps

  • Bent-over kettlebell row (12 kg): 4x12 reps per arm
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell bicep curls (12 kg): 4x15 reps

  • Dips on bar/chair: 4x until failure
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell tricep extensions (8 kg): 4x12-15 reps

Wednesday: Glutes and Abs (High Volume)

  • Kettlebell glute bridge (12-14 kg): 4x20 reps
    • Superset With
  • Bodyweight glute kickbacks: 4x20 reps per leg

  • Side plank with leg raise: 4x30 seconds per side
    • Superset With
  • Mountain climbers: 4x30 seconds

  • HIIT Circuit: 15-20 minutes (Jump rope, fast lunges, side planks)

Thursday: Back and Arms (High Intensity)

  • Pull-ups (unassisted if possible): 4x until failure

  • Kettlebell rows (14 kg): 4x12 reps per arm
    • Superset With
  • Concentrated bicep curls (8-12 kg): 4x12 reps

  • Kettlebell tricep kickbacks (8 kg): 4x15 reps
    • Superset With
  • Kettlebell lateral raises (6-8 kg): 4x12-15 reps

Friday: Full Body Metabolic Circuit

  • Kettlebell swings (12-14 kg): 4x20 reps

  • Bodyweight jump squats: 4x15 reps

  • Push-ups with shoulder taps: 4x until failure

  • Kettlebell clean & press (12 kg): 4x12 reps

  • Burpees: 4x12 reps
